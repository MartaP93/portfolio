OUR RESTAURANT

The Touché was opened in 1985 by a great chef Gordon Amaro who is well known for being first chef awarded with 10 Michelin Stars. Since then was unquestioned top restaurant in France, visited by many celebrities and politics. It's a place where proffesional attitiude meets with a love for food.

Awarded Chefs

Gordon Amaro is not the only famous chef working in Touché. There are many cooking artists in the kitchen waiting to make you dinner perfect in every detail.
